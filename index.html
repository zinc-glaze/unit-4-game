Game Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on each of the crystals, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by choosing a sequence of crystals that makes your total score equal the random number. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts, the values of the four crystals will change.
